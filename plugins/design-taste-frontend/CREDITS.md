## Note on scope

Per the skill's own **Section 13 (Out of Scope)**, this skill targets landing pages, portfolios, and marketing/redesign surfaces — **not** dashboards, dense product UI, or data tables. For those it points to mature design systems (Fluent, Carbon, Atlassian, Polaris) and data-grid libraries (TanStack Table, AG Grid). Its universal anti-slop discipline (the AI-tell bans in Section 9, the em-dash ban in 9.G, the consistency locks, accessibility/contrast and reduced-motion rules) still transfers to any frontend.
