Six verbs over the Pipelines API.

Auth loads from BB_USER and BB_TOKEN or your ~/.bitbucket file, and the workspace and repo can come straight from your git remote. Then it is one word plus a UUID.

  • list and status read recent runs and one run's state.
  • steps and log drill into each step and its output.
  • trigger and stop start or halt a run, with a confirm first.
