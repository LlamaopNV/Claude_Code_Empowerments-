Run it on your own skill.

anvil works on any skill, subagent, or plugin. It generates a balanced suite, runs the A/B in-session on your subscription, scores it, and proposes edits you approve before they land.

  • Activation precision, recall, and F1 from the real transcripts.
  • A judged quality delta with a 95 percent confidence interval.
  • An improvement loop that re-runs and reports the before and after.

3 skills: generating-test-data, running-an-eval, improving-an-artifact.
4 subagents: anvil-analyst, anvil-judge, anvil-task-runner, anvil-testdata-generator.
