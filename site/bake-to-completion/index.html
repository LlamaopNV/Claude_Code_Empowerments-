Interview your own idea.

It works on any early-stage software or product concept. It never batches questions, never writes the plan for you, and the adversarial stress-test is mandatory. The output is one clean brief, then a handoff to design.

  • Rates all 8 dimensions, then drills only the shaky and missing ones.
  • Steelmans the strongest objection and names the assumption that kills it.
  • Saves a strengthened brief, then hands off to brainstorming and planning.

The brief is saved to ./idea-briefs/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md, ready to feed any design process.
